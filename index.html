ACAVAN CAVE
Brgy. Acaban, Culaba.
The Biliran island province boasts a number of sea caves but perhaps the most famous one is located in the Brgy. Acaban, Culaba. The Acaban Cave or better known as Lover’s Cave, open to crystal blue waters where you can take a leisurely swim without getting smashed on a big waves on a good day.

AGTA BEACH RESORT
Talahid, Almeria
Agta Beach Resort in Brgy. Talahid, Almeria, Biliran is conveniently located near the most-visited islands, falls, and destinations in the province. The resort has room accommodations, restaurant, swimming pools, boat for hire, and a dive shop.

DALUTAN ISLAND
Almeria, Biliran
Dalutan Island. This thin strip of pristine white sand beach is shaded by overhanging trees. Sheltered from the open sea, it is great for snorkeling and diving, with a spectacular view of nearby islands.

HIGATANGAN ISLAND
Naval, Biliran
Higatangan Island is a part of Naval Municipality that is separated by a narrow channel located in the north west tip of Leyte. The name Higatangan is derived from the word “atangan” meaning to wait for. Old folks say that the place was where the pirates ambush approaching ships laden with goods and also the place where the Moros lay in wait for their enemies going to Sumbul (Naval).

SAMBAWAN ISLAND
Maripipi, Biliran
Sambawan Island is a small uninhabited land just off the coast of the municipality of Maripipi in Biliran, Leyte. Many say it is the province’s paradise, as it boasts of its undeniable natural beauty that has been attracting hundreds of tourists these days and thus fast becoming the most go-to destination of visitors coming from in and out of the country.

TINAGO FALLS
Caibiran, Biliran
Tinago Waterfalls. The most hypnotic waterfalls magnificently lay its beauty in the heart of Caibiran rainforest, a loop trail taking 10 minutes from the highway. A hideaway that blends perfectly for nature lovers; rich blend of trees, vines, & other exotic plants accompanied by the cooling & enchanting sounds of rushing streams.
